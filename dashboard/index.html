Help & Documentation

How to use Go Academics

Quick Start Guide
1Go to Data Entry and register each student using their anonymised ID (e.g. S001).
2Record Attendance weekly. The system flags absences automatically from week 1.
3Record CA Scores (out of 30) from week 4 onward. The ML model activates at week 7.
4Check Alerts daily for High and Medium risk students and act on recommendations.
The 4-Phase Early Warning Model
Phase 1 (Weeks 1–3): Rule-based. Flags if absences > 2 days.
Phase 2 (Weeks 4–6): Rule-based. Flags if CA < 15/30 or attendance < 80%.
Phase 3 (Weeks 7–9): XGBoost ML first full prediction. Medium/High risk alert.
Phase 4 (Weeks 10–12): Final ML risk score + concrete intervention recommendation.
Risk Level Meanings
HIGH RISK Immediate action needed. Schedule one-on-one or parent meeting.
MEDIUM RISK Monitor closely. Review CA and attendance next week.
LOW RISK Performing well. Continue regular monitoring.
FAQs
Does this work without internet?
Yes. All records are saved locally on this device. Sync happens automatically when a connection is found.
What student IDs do I use?
Use anonymised codes only — e.g. S001, S002. Never record real names.
Why do alerts reset when I click "Mark all read"?
Alerts come back when you navigate away and return — they are always generated from live DB data.
